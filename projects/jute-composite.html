Sodium Silicate–Treated Jute Fiber Reinforced Epoxy Composites

Supervisors: Dr. Md. Abdullah Al Bari, Dr. Md. Ashraful Islam
Institution: Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET)

Overview

This project investigated the effect of sodium silicate treatment on the mechanical, thermal, and fire performance of jute fiber–reinforced epoxy composites.

Methods

  • Composite fabrication and curing
  • Mechanical and thermal testing
  • Fire resistance evaluation
  • SEM-based microstructural analysis

Outcomes

  • Improved fiber–matrix adhesion
  • Enhanced thermal and fire performance
  • Published in Journal of Sandwich Structures and Materials (2025)
